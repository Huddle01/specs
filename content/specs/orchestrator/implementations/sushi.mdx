---
title: Sushi
description: Official Implementation of Orchestrator written in TypeScript and C++ .
---

Sushi is the official implementation of the Huddle01 Orchestrator, 
written in TypeScript and C++. It functions as the core management system 
for media nodes within the Huddle01 decentralized real-time communication (dRTC) platform. 
Sushi is designed to optimize the allocation of resources, manage media traffic, 
and provide essential services to ensure seamless and secure video conferencing.
