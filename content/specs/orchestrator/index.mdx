title: General
description: Introduction of the Orchestrator
---

The Orchestrator component in Huddle01 for managing media resources and client connections. This 
component ensures robust client connectivity, and efficient media resource allocation.

Key responsibilities are:

 - **Allocating Media-Nodes**: Assigns appropriate media-nodes to clients based on availability and Geographical proximity.
 - **Facilitating WebRTC Connections**: Ensures the establishment of WebRTC connections between clients and their allocated media-nodes.
 - **Registry Integration**: Interfaces with the registry to track media node resources.
